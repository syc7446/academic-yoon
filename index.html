About me

I am a Ph.D. student in the Bradley Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Virginia Tech. I am a part of RAAS Lab advised by Prof. Pratap Tokekar. I received my M.S. and B.S. degrees in the School of Mechanical Engineering from Korea University in 2013 and 2011, respectively.

Research

My research interests include

  • Algorithmic Robotics

  • Path Planning

  • Computational Geometry

  • Cooperative Robotics

  • Decision Making
